The Postfeminist Ad Generator is a satirical take on the language of corporate empowerment. Using procedural text generation, it mimics the empty slogans of marketing campaigns that claim to uplift women while ultimately reinforcing capitalist ideals.

By remixing common phrases from branding and self-help culture, this project exposes the way feminist language is often commodified into aesthetic, consumable products rather than meaningful change.

Inspired by algorithmic advertising, this generator highlights the tension between genuine empowerment and the forces of marketing that seek to profit from it. Some word choices and structures were generated with assistance from ChatGPT, reflecting the ways AI can both reinforce and critique digital culture.
